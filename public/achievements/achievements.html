Achievements

Dean's List

  • To obtain the Dean's list, a student must maintain above a 3.5 GPA at the end of the semester
  • I have obtained this status for 50% of the total semesters that I have been at Boston University

Game Jam

  • A Game Jam is an event where, with a team, you must develop a video game in a short time period
  • I have participated and placed highly in a variety of these events, sometimes making a game in as fast as 48 hours
  • This achievement forced me to be adaptable, a fast learner, and a good teammate

Hackathon

  • I have participated in various Hackathons, while similar to Game Jams, the conditions in these events are quite different.
  • These competitions are normally shorter, sometimes confined to a single day, and I am often partnered with strangers
  • Placing well in these competitions taught me what it takes to develop efficient and valuable software
